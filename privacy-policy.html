KorschSales – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2025

KorschSales is an internal sales tool developed by and for KORSCH AG. This app is distributed exclusively to authorized KORSCH employees and sales representatives via Apple TestFlight. It is not available to the general public.

1. Information We Collect

KorschSales does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data entered or generated within the app remains on your device.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • A hashed (SHA-256) app password for screen lock authentication
  • CPO (Certified Pre-Owned) equipment inventory data
  • User preferences and app settings

2. Biometric Authentication (Face ID)

KorschSales offers Face ID as an optional convenience to unlock the app. Biometric data is processed entirely by Apple's built-in LocalAuthentication framework on your device. KORSCH AG never has access to your biometric data. Face ID can be disabled at any time in your device's Settings.

3. Inventory Synchronization

Authorized administrators may publish CPO inventory updates to a private, access-controlled repository. This data contains only equipment listings (machine models, specifications, and pricing). No personal user data is ever uploaded or synchronized.

4. Data Sharing

KORSCH AG does not sell, trade, or share any data collected by this app with third parties. The app does not include any advertising SDKs, analytics services, or third-party tracking tools.

5. Data Security

App access is protected by a device password and optional Face ID. Sensitive values (such as the access PIN) are stored only as cryptographic hashes and cannot be reversed. We recommend keeping your device updated with the latest iOS security patches.

6. Children's Privacy

KorschSales is intended solely for use by KORSCH AG employees and business partners. It is not directed at children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

KORSCH AG may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any revised policy.
